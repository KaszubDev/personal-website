## Educational school trips directory

This project is a comprehensive search and booking platform for school trips and educational tours. It focuses on presenting a massive database of travel offers in an intuitive, easy-to-navigate manner.

* **UX for large data sets:** With hundreds of offers, the main challenge was preventing "choice paralysis".
